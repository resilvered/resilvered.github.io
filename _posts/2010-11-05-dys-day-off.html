A quick run down on events for today, more to follow, maybe.

First stop Cowgirl Creamery in Petaluma, turned out just to be the office, oops.

Second stop The Twit Cottage, a geek's indigence, I listen to some podcasts from these guys. Had a brief chat with Alex Lindsay outside the cottage and he suggested we might have better luck with the Cowgirl Creamery in Point Reyes, lucky we were heading that way.

Stop three, Point Reyes Lighthouse, here is a tip , don't go there on a bad weather day, apparently only the second foggiest place in North America. We had a good weather day and there was limited visibility, oh and take a jacket, or jumper, and be prepared for a 30 storey climb back up.

Stop Four, lunch at Station House Cafe at Point Reyes, followed by a visit to the Cowgirl Creamery nice cheese.

Last stop for the day was the small town of Graton just west of Santa Rosa for an afternoon beverage.
